FAQ


Q1. Why should I choose deccanrealty.com?
DECCANREALTY.COM is Delhi-NCR's number one property portal, deals with every aspect of the consumers' needs in the real estate industry. It is an online forum where buyers, sellers, Renters and brokers/agents can exchange information about real estate properties quickly, effectively and inexpensively. At deccanrealty.com you can advertise a property, search for a property, browse through properties.
Q2. In which cities do you offer your services?
We offer our services in Delhi, Noida, Ghaziabad, Gurugram, and Faridabad.
Q3. How can I Sign Up on DECCANREALTY.COM?
Sign Up on DECCANREALTY.COMis a matter of a few steps. You just log onto the website deccanrealty.com, fill in your credentials, and click SIGN UP, and you are done. Simple!
Q4. Are email id and mobile number manadatory to sign up a profile?
Yes, they are. You need a valid email id and a mobile number to create a profile. Each profile has a distinctive email id. An email id already registered with deccanrealty.com can't be used for another profile sign up. However, we are flexible enough to allow you to change your email id and mobile number within the created profile.
Q5. I forgot my password. is there any option to reset password?
It is a simple process. You can reset the password by clicking on 'Forget Password.' Then, recover your password as you would do on any other password resetting option on the internet. For changing the password, you can go to the 2nd step of login option, give your current password, and create a new one. That's all.
Q6. How much time does it take to make my profile visible in the search list?
All it takes is 1 – 2 hours. At this time, we perform a basic screening process at our end to ensure our customer's security. The screening includes going through the content you provided and pictures attached with your stuff. Right after your profile qualifies, the screening process, it's made visible.
Q7. What kind of properties are listed on your Website?
We have a range of rental flats, apartments, residential properties, and plots that you can navigate through our detailed videos or mobile app. From new to the rental and resale properties, we cover everything.
Q8. I want to sell my property. What are the documents buyers would require from me?
Following are the documents you will need while selling a property.
  • Letter of allotment
  • Sale deed
  • Sanctioned plan
  • Encumbrance certificate
  • Sale agreement
Q9. Who needs to pay the stamp duty? The seller OR the buyer?
It's always the buyer who pays the stamp duty.
